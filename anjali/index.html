PAPER QUILLING

Paper Quilling is also known as Paper Filigree. It is an art of rolling, scrolling, shaping or fringing of narrow strips of paper to create amazing designs and compositions.

Quilling is a very old craft form which has gained popularity all over the world in recent times. From a personal of view, Quilling is an art of preparing innovative,creative and minute paper designs.It involves cutting strips of paper, giving them various shapes/forms and sticking them together to form artistic designs.It also involves use of the quilling tool. .
